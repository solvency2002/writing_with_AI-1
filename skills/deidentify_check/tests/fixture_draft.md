## Case presentation

A 92-year-old male farmer from Maebashi city, Gunma prefecture, was admitted to
Gunma University Hospital on 2024-03-15 with a 3-day history of fever and dyspnea.
His medical record number was MRN 1234567 (case ID: A-22-0815).

His wife, a 45-year-old nurse at the same hospital, brought him in. The patient
had been diagnosed with a rare hereditary myopathy (combined with his occupation
as a sericulture farmer and residence in the Kanra district) in 2023年4月12日.

On March 18, 2024, chest CT showed bilateral consolidation. The patient was
transferred to the ICU the following day. By 2024-03-25, he had been weaned from
mechanical ventilation.

## Clinical course

Day-1 (admission) labs: WBC 18,200/μL, CRP 24.3 mg/dL.
Treatment was started according to local protocol. The case was discussed at the
weekly ID conference on 2024年3月20日.

The 2020 IDSA guidelines were followed for antibiotic selection. The patient
was discharged on 2024-04-02.

## Discussion

This is, to our knowledge, the first reported case of this presentation in an
elderly Japanese sericulture farmer.
